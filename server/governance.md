# Civic & Governance Principles

This system is designed for civic neutrality and regulatory compliance.

## Neutrality
- No political advocacy
- No opinion shaping
- No ranking bias

## Transparency
- AI responses are prompt-derived
- Deterministic processing
- No hidden learning loops

## Auditability
- Stateless execution
- Reproducible inference
- Clear separation of logic and AI output

## Eligibility
- Election Commission of India (ECI) compliant
- Multilingual
- Non-partisan by design
