# Problem Statement Alignment

## Civic Problem

Citizens face fragmented, multilingual, and often ambiguous access to
neutral information about civic processes, public issues, and elections.

This leads to:
- Misinformation
- Unequal access to knowledge
- Reduced civic participation

## Intended Outcome

This system provides **neutral, factual, and multilingual assistance**
to help citizens understand civic processes **without persuasion or advocacy**.

## Non‑Goals (Explicit)

This system does NOT:
- Influence voting decisions
- Rank political parties or candidates
- Recommend political actions
- Shape opinions or sentiment

## Alignment with Civic Principles

- ✅ Informational, not persuasive
- ✅ Neutral tone by design
- ✅ Multilingual access
- ✅ Auditable responses
- ✅ Stateless, replayable inference

## Regulatory Alignment

- Election Commission of India (ECI) neutrality principles
- No campaign messaging
- No targeted political persuasion
